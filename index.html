THE FOREST

You wake up from a severe headache and find yourself in a quiet forest. Your last memory is being knocked out by something while walking on the street. Why are you here? Who kidnapped you?

You started to walk toward the light, but when you get there, there is nothing but dead silence.

Suddenly, you hear a monster roar. When you turned around, it's staring at you.

You saw a light shining from far away, which seemed to be your only hope.

You decided to ignore the light. Then, you see the light getting closer and closer to you. You subconsciously run in the opposite direction, but you are still caught by a hand. "Why don't you come with me?" You hear a voice wiper in your ear.

You keep running forward, and finally, you run into a cave. You saw a body of a dead person inside. Who is that? You don't Know. You left, but you can't get out of the forest.

THE END

You fail, you die.

You climbed up a tree, waiting for the monster to leave, and you survived.

You followed the light all the way out of the forest, and when you thought everything was finally over, you saw a sign on the ground that read: "The final mission: choose a person on the street, take him/her into the forest, and guide him/her out of the forest."
